Obtaining Your License

To obtain a Gurobi 12 license, you will need to visit the Gurobi User Portal. If you are a commercial user under maintenance, you should see your Gurobi 12 license under Licenses. If you would like to request a free academic license, you can do so from the Licenses Request section. Once you have a license in the Gurobi User Portal, you will need to follow the instructions for installing or downloading a license by clicking on the Install or Open button next to your license.
